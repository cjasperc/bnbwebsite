More Than a Shed

Designed to Entertain. Built to Impress.

A Beams & Braces garden bar isn't a converted shed with a shelf in it. It's a properly designed, built-in bar space with a solid counter, integrated electrics for refrigeration and sound, atmospheric lighting, and materials that look as good in ten years as they do on day one.

We design every bar around how you entertain, whether that's a compact indoor-outdoor space for summer evenings, or a larger year-round bar room with full insulation and heating. Each one is bespoke, and each one is the project clients tell us was the best decision they made.

Feature Highlights

Everything That Makes It Special

Built-In Bar Counter

Solid hardwood, granite, or composite counter surface, built in, not sat on top. We include a footrail, bottle storage, and a top-mount bar sink as standard in our full spec. Feels like a pub. Works better.

Atmospheric Lighting

Layered lighting design: warm LED downlights for the bar area, LED strip under the counter, pendant lights for character, and exterior spots on the decking or patio. All on separate circuits, set the mood.

Sound System Wiring

We run speaker cable to your specified positions as part of the build, you choose the speakers, we make sure the wiring is in the right place before the walls go on. Includes an outdoor rated speaker point.

Fridge & Refrigeration

Dedicated 13A circuit for under-counter fridge or wine cooler. We also wire for a keg fridge or dual-zone cooler if that's the plan. Beer on tap is absolutely achievable, and we've done it.

Premium Cladding

Composite cladding that matches your decking, or western red cedar for a warm, publike feel. We can also render the exterior if you prefer a contemporary, rendered finish that blends with your home.

Integrated Decking Area

Every garden bar benefits from an outdoor seating area. We design and install the composite decking or patio as part of the same project, so it all fits, drains correctly, and looks intentional.

How It Works

From Brief to Bar in Five Stages

01

Design Brief

We talk through how you entertain, what drinks you serve, how many guests, and what kind of atmosphere you want. The design follows the brief.

02

Design & Quote

Sketched layout, elevation drawings, material specification, and a fixed price. Everything itemised, structure, joinery, electrics, lighting, and decking area.

03

Groundworks

Concrete pad for the bar building, plus sub-base for decking or patio. We coordinate both so the levels work perfectly together.

04

Build & Fit-Out

Frame, cladding, roofing, joinery, electrics, lighting, plumbing point, counter installation, and decking, all on one programme, one team.

05

Handover

Full walkthrough of lighting circuits, appliance connections, and controls. Electrical certificate and warranty. First round is on you.
