Thinking About Paving?

Composite decking vs paving, the honest comparison for a Burnham Market property.

Paving is familiar. But for coastal second homes at this level, the long-term case points clearly elsewhere.

Composite Decking Paving / Patio
Aesthetic longevity 25-year warranty on colour and finish. Looks intentional for the life of the build. Paving weathers visibly on the north Norfolk coast within 3–5 years. Salt air leaves marks; algae becomes a persistent presence.
Maintenance An occasional rinse. No contractor required, no annual schedule. Annual jet washing, re-pointing, and moss treatment. More frequent near the coast.
Second home suitability Returns unchanged after months of vacancy. No deterioration during unoccupied periods. Weed growth in joints, algae on surface, visible deterioration during extended absence is guaranteed in the coast's damp climate.
Finish quality Trex Transcend delivers a genuinely premium result, not recognisably different from high-end timber on first view. Quality varies significantly. Even high-spec paving looks dated faster than composite in coastal conditions.
Property value contribution A well-specified deck adds visibly to how a property presents, useful at resale and for short-let positioning. Standard paving rarely differentiates a property at this level. Worn coastal paving actively detracts.
