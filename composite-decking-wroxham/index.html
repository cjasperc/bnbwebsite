Outdoor Living on the Broads

Waterside gardens need materials that can handle it.

The Broads are stunning. They're also persistently damp. The combination of high groundwater, frequent fog, and properties that sit near or on the water creates the worst possible conditions for timber and most paving. Rot, algae, mould, and slippery surfaces are the norm, not the exception.

Composite decking was designed for exactly this environment. Moisture-resistant, slip-resistant, and requiring nothing beyond an occasional clean, it's the only outdoor surface that genuinely suits a waterside property in Norfolk.

  • Moisture-resistant by design

    Trex boards won't absorb water, warp, or develop the algae growth that makes riverside timber so dangerous in winter.

  • TrexPRO Platinum installer

    Norfolk's only TrexPRO Platinum certified team. The highest installer certification Trex awards, your warranty is fully protected.

  • Perfect for holiday lets

    Broads holiday lets compete on lifestyle. A high-quality deck, particularly with river or broad views, commands a meaningful premium in nightly rates.

Considering a Patio Instead?

Why waterside properties choose decking over paving.

Paving works in some settings. On the Broads, with high moisture, high usage, and properties often left unoccupied through winter, it tends to underperform significantly.

Composite Decking Paving / Patio
Damp conditions Moisture-resistant. No swelling, no rot, no algae growth. Paving absorbs water. Algae establishes rapidly near water. Becomes hazardous by autumn.
Maintenance Occasional rinse. Nothing else required, even after months unoccupied. Weeds, moss, and re-pointing. Particularly bad when left through a Broads winter.
Holiday let appeal Decking with a water view is a guest favourite. Directly impacts listing photos and nightly rates. Worn or mossy paving undermines a premium listing price.
Lifespan 25+ year warranty. No replacement boards, no re-laying. 10–15 years before subsidence or cracking requires work. Waterside ground movement accelerates this.
Safety Slip-resistant even when wet. Critical near water for guests of all ages. Smooth or algae-covered paving near water is a genuine safety risk.
