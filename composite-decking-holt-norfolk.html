Outdoor Living in Holt

Your garden should work as hard as your property does.

Holt and the north Norfolk coast attract homeowners who value quality. Whether it's a permanent residence off the High Street, a holiday let near Sheringham, or a weekend retreat close to the AONB, the outdoor space is part of what makes the property worth owning.

The problem is that traditional timber decking, and most paving, doesn't hold up in this climate. The damp Norfolk winters, coastal air, and long periods of neglect between visits mean most surfaces look tired within three or four years. We install composite decking that doesn't.

  • Ideal for holiday homes

    No maintenance needed between visits. Your deck will look exactly the same in October as it did in June.

  • 25+ year lifespan

    Trex boards carry a 25-year fade and stain warranty. No repainting, no re-staining, no replacement boards.

  • Built for damp coastal climates

    Composite doesn't absorb moisture, rot, or develop the algae and mould that make timber and stone dangerously slippery.

  • TrexPRO Platinum installed

    We're Norfolk's only TrexPRO Platinum installer, the highest certification Trex awards. Your warranty is protected.

Why Composite Wins in North Norfolk

The coast is hard on outdoor materials. Composite handles it.

North Norfolk has more rainfall than the UK average. Salt-laden sea air accelerates rust and rot. Properties occupied part-time can go months without any maintenance. These conditions expose the weaknesses in timber and paving that don't show up on a sunny July afternoon, but they're visible by February.

Thinking About Paving?

Composite decking vs paving, the honest comparison.

Paving is popular for a reason, it's familiar. But for coastal and second-home properties in particular, the long-term picture looks very different.

Composite Decking Paving / Patio
Maintenance Rinse once or twice a year. Nothing else required. Annual weeding, re-pointing, and pressure washing. Moss and algae build quickly in damp climates.
Lifespan 25+ years with a manufacturer warranty. 10–15 years before cracking, subsidence, or discolouration requires significant work.
Long-term cost Higher upfront, but near-zero running cost. Cheaper over 15+ years. Lower upfront but ongoing maintenance costs add up. Re-laying is expensive.
Coastal & damp conditions Moisture-resistant. Doesn't rot, swell, or grow mould. Paving absorbs water. Salt air causes efflorescence. Algae makes surfaces dangerously slippery in winter.
Safety (wet) Textured, slip-resistant surface even when soaking wet. Smooth or polished paving becomes hazardous when wet, especially with moss growth.
Visual impact Creates zones, levels, and a defined outdoor living space. Photographs beautifully. Flat, uniform surface. Limited design flexibility. Ages visibly in coastal climates.
Holiday let advantage Premium-looking decking commands higher nightly rates. Guests book on lifestyle photos. Paving rarely adds to listing appeal. Worn paving actively undermines it.
Unoccupied periods Needs nothing. Returns looking exactly as you left it. Weeds establish in joints rapidly. Six months unvisited means significant work on arrival.
