Why Enhance?

Smart Performance. Honest Sustainability.

Trex Enhance Naturals sits at the heart of the Trex range, delivering exceptional performance without the premium price tag. Its natural woodgrain embossing and earthy colour palette blend seamlessly with gardens, while the dual-sided boards give you extra design flexibility.

With 95% recycled content (reclaimed wood and plastic film), Enhance is one of the most eco-conscious decking choices on the market, and one of the lowest-maintenance. No staining, no sealing, no annual treatments. Just a wipe-down when needed.

Common Questions

Trex Enhance FAQ

Trex Enhance Naturals is the entry-level tier of the Trex range, focusing on a natural wood aesthetic and superb sustainability credentials. Trex Signature sits mid-range with a square-edge, more contemporary profile and a wider colour range. Trex Transcend is the premium tier, deeper embossing, the widest colour and grain selection, and the highest performance cap layer. All three carry the 25-year Trex warranty. We'll help you choose based on your budget and aesthetic goals.

All composite decking, including Trex, will experience some initial weathering in the first 6–12 weeks as the board settles and any surface tannins wash off. After that, the colour stabilises and remains protected by the 25-year fade warranty. The natural tones of Enhance Naturals are specifically designed to weather gracefully rather than look faded.

Yes. Enhance Naturals boards have a natural woodgrain emboss on both faces. This is a genuine installation feature, it reduces waste during cuts and gives you flexibility if you ever want to refresh a heavily-worn area. That said, once installed with hidden fixings, flipping boards in situ isn't practical, the benefit is primarily at the installation stage.

Yes, Trex Enhance Naturals can be used on elevated decks. The key consideration for any raised structure is the subframe: joist sizing, bearer spans, and post footings must be correctly engineered for the height involved. We handle all structural design as part of our installation service and can supply structural calculations if required by your local authority.

A brush-down to remove leaves and debris, followed by a rinse with warm soapy water, is all you need for routine maintenance. For tougher marks, algae, bird droppings, food stains, Trex CleanSweep cleaner works brilliantly. Never use a pressure washer above 1500 psi or hold the nozzle closer than 8 inches from the board surface. We provide full care instructions at handover.
