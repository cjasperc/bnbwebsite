Why Composite?

Less maintenance.
Longer life.
Better looking.

Timber has its charm. But for Norfolk's damp climate and busy households, composite delivers a deck that holds its colour, resists the seasons, and never needs sanding.

  • Built to last 25+ years

    Trex composite decks carry a 25-year fade and stain warranty. Timber typically needs replacing after 10–15 years.

  • No weekend maintenance

    No sanding. No staining. No sealing. A basic clean a few times a year is all it needs.

  • Made from 95% recycled materials

    Responsible manufacturing, better for your garden and better for the planet.

  • Slip-resistant in all conditions

    Textured surface performs in wet Norfolk winters, safe around pools, hot tubs, and high-use areas.
