The Premium Choice

Deep woodgrain. Lasting colour. Zero maintenance.

Trex Transcend is the flagship of the Trex range, engineered for homeowners who want the look of premium hardwood without any of the upkeep. The boards feature Trex's deepest, most realistic woodgrain profile and come in 6 UK colours from warm tropical tones to cool coastal greys.

  • Fade & Stain Resistant

    PermaTuff cap technology protects against UV, moisture, and everyday staining. Colour stays true for decades.

  • Scratch Resistant

    The hardened cap layer resists scratches from furniture, foot traffic, and pets.

  • Splinter-Free

    No splinters, no warping, no rough edges, ever. Safe for bare feet and families.

  • Versatile Installation

    Available in square-edge and grooved profile. Compatible with hidden fasteners for a seamless, nail-free finish.
