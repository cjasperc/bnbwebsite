The Architectural Range

Minimal lines. Maximum impact.

Trex Signature is built for contemporary homes and outdoor spaces where design is everything. The boards feature a smooth surface option alongside a subtle grain, and come in two refined UK colours that complement modern architecture without competing with it.

  • Contemporary Profile

    Slim, clean board profile designed for flush fitting and seamless integration with modern outdoor spaces.

  • Hidden Fasteners

    All Signature boards are grooved for hidden fastening, no visible screws, no surface interruption.

  • Fade & Stain Resistant

    25-year warranty against fading, staining, and scratching. The colour you choose on day one is the colour you'll see in year twenty.

  • Railing Integration

    Designed to pair with Trex's Signature® railing system in charcoal black, a cohesive outdoor design from deck to balustrade.
