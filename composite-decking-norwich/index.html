Why Composite?

Never sand. Never stain. Never replace.

Traditional timber decking needs sanding, staining, and regular treatment to survive Norfolk's damp winters. Composite doesn't. It arrives looking exceptional and stays that way, for decades.

As a TrexPRO Platinum installer, the highest certification Trex awards, we supply and fit the full range of Trex composite boards across Norwich and Norfolk.

  • 25+ year lifespan

    Trex boards carry a 25-year fade and stain warranty. Most timber decks need replacing after 10–15 years.

  • No annual maintenance

    No sanding, staining, or sealing. A couple of basic cleans per year is all it takes.

  • 95% recycled materials

    Every Trex board is made from 95% recycled wood and plastic film, a responsible choice for your home.

  • Slip-resistant surface

    Textured boards grip in wet conditions, safe around pools, hot tubs, and high-traffic areas.

Our Certification

What TrexPRO Platinum actually means.

Trex runs a three-tier installer programme: TrexPRO, TrexPRO Plus, and TrexPRO Platinum. Platinum is the highest tier, awarded only to installers who demonstrate consistent installation standards, technical training, and a high volume of completed projects.

As a Platinum-accredited team, we receive direct technical support from Trex, access to the full product range, and our installations are eligible for Trex's extended workmanship warranty on top of the standard 25-year product warranty. It's not a label we bought, it's one we earned project by project.

  • Three tiers: TrexPRO, Plus, Platinum

    Platinum is the top tier. Fewer than 5% of Trex installers in the UK hold this certification.

  • Extended workmanship warranty

    Platinum installers carry a workmanship warranty alongside Trex's 25-year product warranty.

  • Direct Trex technical support

    We have direct access to Trex's technical team, meaning faster solutions if anything unusual comes up on your project.
